Welcome to SilkSphere

We’ve created an innovative web platform designed to support silkworm farmers in managing their rearing house conditions and detecting silkworm diseases. By leveraging advanced machine learning (ML) techniques and IoT sensor integration, we enable users to easily upload images of silkworms for disease analysis. We also provide real-time monitoring of essential environmental factors, such as temperature, humidity, and CO2 levels.
